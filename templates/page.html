Search-to-Action Journey

Seven layers. One governed path from intent to outcome.

  2. 02 Visibility Presence in relevant results at the right moment.
  3. 03 Experience The quality of interaction after arrival.
  4. 04 Trust The user’s assessment of credibility and authority.
  5. 05 Navigation Confident movement through the site architecture.
  6. 06 Action The specific, measurable response taken.
  7. 07 Strategic Outcome Downstream consequence of repeated successful journeys.
