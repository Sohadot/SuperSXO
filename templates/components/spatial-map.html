Search-to-Action Journey

Seven layers governing the path from search intent to strategic outcome.
  1. 01 Search Intent The motivation that initiates the search.
  2. 02 Visibility The degree to which the asset appears in relevant search results.
  3. 03 Experience The quality of interaction after the user arrives.
  4. 04 Trust The user’s assessment of credibility and authority.
  5. 05 Navigation The user’s ability to move through the site with confidence.
  6. 06 Action The specific, measurable response taken by the user.
  7. 07 Strategic Outcome The downstream consequence of repeated successful journeys.
