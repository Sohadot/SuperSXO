Examination Record

The Seven Layers Under Examination

Each layer represents a governing condition of the search-to-action journey. The SuperSXO Framework examines whether each condition is satisfied.

01
Search Intent Under Examination

Does the asset address the motivation that precedes the query?

Search Intent governs every subsequent layer. The query is a signal, not the governing condition. What the visitor intended before they typed determines what result will satisfy them. Intent misalignment at this layer propagates failure through all layers that follow.

02
Visibility Under Examination

Is this asset findable at the right moment?

Visibility is the entry condition. Without it, no subsequent layer can function. It is necessary but not sufficient. Presence in results at the wrong intent intersection produces no journey. The gate must be open at the right moment, not simply open.

03
Experience Under Examination

Does the page deliver on the search promise?

Experience is the proof point of the visibility claim. Speed, coherence, and relevance assessed within moments of arrival. A broken promise here terminates the journey regardless of how strong the visibility was. Experience failure is the most immediate form of journey loss.

04
Trust Confirmed

Does the visitor accept the authority of this source?

Trust cannot be declared. It is demonstrated through evidence, coherence, and consistency. The threshold condition for the journey to reach action. A single inconsistency can collapse this layer. Trust is the most commonly absent layer in standard SEO practice.

05
Navigation Under Examination

Can the visitor identify what comes next?

Navigation means path confidence: the visitor's ability to understand where they are, what the next step is, and what happens if they take it. Navigation failure terminates the journey regardless of how well the prior four layers performed. Architecture is not decoration.

06
Action Ready

Is the terminal action clear and achievable from here?

Action is the specific, measurable terminal event the journey was designed to produce. It is only reachable when all preceding layers are functioning. A high arrival rate with a low action rate indicates breakdown in one or more upstream layers. Action is the output of the system, not the input to optimize.

07
Strategic Outcome Registered

Did the journey produce downstream strategic value?

Strategic Outcome is the downstream consequence of the action. Not what analytics records — what the asset was built to achieve. This layer separates sovereign asset thinking from ordinary website thinking. The outcome register is the reason every other layer of examination exists.
