# SuperSXO — Domain Cluster Strategy

## Governing Rule

> **No duplicate sites. No fragmented authority. No competing content clusters.**

---

## Domain Cluster Map

| Domain | Role | Strategic Function |
|---|---|---|
| **SuperSXO.com** | Primary authority | Main brand, canonical asset, framework, methodology, diagnostic system, and long-term strategic property |
| **SXOSolution.com** | Commercial gateway | Solution-oriented traffic, diagnostic offers, audit requests, reports, and future service/product pathways |
| **SuperSXO.info** | Informational routing | Defensive and explanatory routing toward reference content such as `/what-is-sxo/` or `/sxo-framework/` |
| **SXOSolution.info** | Solution explanation layer | Defensive routing toward `/sxo-audit/`, `/sxo-solution/`, or equivalent commercial explanation pages |
| **SuperSXO.link** | Campaign routing | Controlled routing for framework, score, methodology, and campaign links |
| **SXOSolution.link** | Action routing | Controlled routing for audit, report, diagnostic, and action-oriented links |

---

## Authority Rule

> **SuperSXO.com owns authority.**
> **SXOSolution.com carries commercial intent.**
> **.info explains.**
> **.link routes.**

---

## Domain Role Restrictions

### SuperSXO.com

- Canonical owner of all SXO content
- All other cluster domains route toward it, not away from it
- No duplicate content may exist on other domains
- All canonical tags across the cluster must point here

### SXOSolution.com

- Commercial and service-oriented only
- Does not compete with or duplicate SuperSXO.com content
- Offers link to SuperSXO.com for authority content

### .info Domains

- Defensive registrations only
- Routes to canonical SuperSXO.com pages
- No independent content published
- No independent SEO campaigns

### .link Domains

- Routing layer only
- No content pages
- Used for campaign tracking and controlled redirect paths only

---

## Cluster Integrity Rules

1. No domain in the cluster may create content that competes with SuperSXO.com
2. All external links pointing to non-.com domains must be confirmed to route to SuperSXO.com
3. Canonical tags must always point to SuperSXO.com pages
4. No cluster domain may have a separate social media presence without explicit approval
5. All cluster domains must use Cloudflare as the DNS and edge layer

---

## Future Expansion Governance

New domain acquisitions must:

- be reviewed against the cluster strategy
- have a defined role before acquisition
- be logged in `DECISION_LOG.md`
- not be acquired speculatively without a documented strategic function

---

## Domain Cluster and Buyer Logic

The cluster reinforces acquisition value by demonstrating:

- controlled category presence
- defensive registration discipline
- structured routing architecture
- no fragmented or abandoned properties
- a coherent multi-domain strategy under one authority
